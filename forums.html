PushSquare

Formed in 2009 by experienced writer Sammy Barker, and expanded by the team behind the massively popular Nintendo Life, Push Square is a totally independent publication, providing honest, unbiased opinions on everything PlayStation related.

Playstation Universe

Launched in October 2006, PlayStation Universe (PSU.com) is the world's largest independent PlayStation website, primarily known for its exclusive news and reviews for PlayStation platforms. The site features up to the minute previews, features, interviews, as well as a wide variety of multimedia content.
